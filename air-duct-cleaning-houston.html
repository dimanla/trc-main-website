Our NADCA-Certified Cleaning Process

At Texas Right Choice, we don't just "vacuum out" the vents. We follow a comprehensive, NADCA-based cleaning protocol to ensure every inch of your system is sanitized. Our process includes:

  • Inspection: We use high-resolution cameras to identify the specific layout of your ductwork and locate heavy buildup areas.
  • Negative Pressure Extraction: Our powerful vacuum systems create high-velocity suction that pulls all contaminants away from your living spaces.
  • Mechanical Agitation: We utilize specialized brushes and compressed air tools to dislodge stubborn debris from the duct walls.
  • Component Cleaning: We clean the blower motor, coils, and air handler components to ensure the entire system is free of particulates.
  • Sanitization: We apply EPA-approved antimicrobial treatments to eliminate mold spores and prevent future growth.

When Should You Schedule a Cleaning?

While most homes benefit from cleaning every 1 to 2 years, certain factors in Houston, Texas may require more frequent service. You should consider an immediate cleaning if:

  • You have pets that shed dander or hair.
  • Family members suffer from chronic allergies or respiratory issues.
  • You have recently completed a home renovation or remodeling project.
  • You notice visible dust plumes coming from vents.
  • There is a persistent musty odor in your home.
  • You have recently moved into a new home and aren't sure of the HVAC history.

What is included in a professional air duct cleaning?

A comprehensive cleaning includes all supply and return ducts, registers, grills, and the main plenum box. We also perform a visual inspection of your furnace and blower motor to ensure the entire system is free of debris.

How long does the air duct cleaning process take?

Most residential projects take between 3 to 5 hours, depending on the size of the home and the complexity of the ductwork layout. We take our time to ensure a thorough deep clean.

Do I need to be home during the air duct cleaning?

Yes, we recommend an adult be present to allow access to all areas and to review the before-and-after results with our technician. This ensures all your specific concerns are addressed.

Will air duct cleaning make a mess in my house?

No, we use high-power HEPA negative pressure systems that contain all dust and debris within our equipment. Our technicians also use drop cloths and shoe covers to protect your living spaces.

How do you protect my floors and furniture?

We use protective floor runners, corner guards for our hoses, and plastic sheeting where necessary to ensure your home remains exactly as we found it, but with cleaner air.

Is the equipment you use loud?

Our powerful vacuum systems are powerful but stayed outside. The portable equipment used indoors is similar to a heavy-duty vacuum in terms of noise level, so it won't be excessively disruptive.

Do you clean the entire HVAC system or just the ducts?

We clean the entire air path, including the supply ducts, return air ducts, and the components of the air handler. We also recommend periodic coil cleaning for maximum efficiency.

Are the cleaning products you use safe for children and pets?

Absolutely. We use EPA-approved, non-toxic antimicrobial treatments that are specifically designed for HVAC systems and are safe for all household members, including pets.

Can air duct cleaning remove cigarette smoke odors?

Yes, removing the smoke particles and residue trapped inside the ducts is a critical step in eliminating persistent odors. Our sanitization process helps neutralize the smell at the source.

Will cleaning my ducts stop my smoke alarms from going off?

If your alarms are being triggered by fine dust particles circulating from the vents, a deep cleaning will significantly reduce particulate matter and resolve that specific issue.

Should I change my air filter after the cleaning is done?

Yes, we always recommend installing a fresh, high-quality filter immediately after the cleaning to ensure your newly sanitized system remains clean and efficient.

How can I maintain clean ducts between professional visits?

Using high-efficiency filters, changing them every 3 months, and keeping your home's vacuuming and dusting routine will help minimize the amount of debris that enters your system.

Do you provide a report or photos of the results?

Yes, our technicians take before-and-after photos of your system's interior to provide visual proof of the cleaning results. We want you to see the difference for yourself.

What certifications do your technicians hold?

Our technicians are trained according to NADCA standards and hold various industry certifications to ensure they provide the highest level of professional service.

Do you offer residential and commercial duct cleaning services?

Yes, Texas Right Choice provides professional duct cleaning for single-family homes, apartments, and commercial buildings of all sizes across the Greater Houston area.
