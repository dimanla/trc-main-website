AC Repair FAQs

How much does a typical AC repair cost in Houston?

Repair costs vary depending on the issue. Minor repairs like capacitor replacement or drain clearing are affordable, while major component replacements cost more. We provide upfront, flat-rate pricing before any work begins.

Why is my AC running but not blowing cold air?

Common causes include low refrigerant, a dirty evaporator coil, or a failed condenser fan. Turn the system off and call us immediately to prevent expensive compressor damage.

Do you offer emergency AC repair on weekends and holidays?

Yes, Texas Right Choice operates 24/7/365. We know Houston heat doesn't take breaks, so neither do our expert technicians.

How long will it take for a technician to arrive?

We pride ourselves on rapid response times. In most cases, we can have a certified technician at your Greater Houston home within 60 to 90 minutes of your emergency call.

Is it better to repair or replace my old air conditioner?

A good rule of thumb is to consider the age and efficiency of the system. If repairs are becoming frequent and the unit is over 10-12 years old, a new high-efficiency system might save you more in the long run. We'll provide an honest assessment of both options.

What are the signs that my AC needs immediate repair?

Strange noises (grinding or squealing), unusual odors (burning or musty), frequent cycling, or a sudden spike in your energy bills are all signs that your system needs professional attention.

Can you fix all major AC brands?

Absolutely. Our technicians are trained to service all major manufacturers, including Trane, Carrier, Lennox, Goodman, Rheem, and York, among many others.

Why is my AC unit freezing up or covered in ice?

This is usually caused by restricted airflow (dirty filters or blocked returns) or low refrigerant levels. Turn the system off to let it thaw and call us for a diagnostic.

How often should my air conditioner be serviced?

In the Houston climate, we recommend professional maintenance twice a year—once in the spring before the heat hits, and once in the fall before the heating season.

Are your technicians licensed and insured?

Yes, all Texas Right Choice technicians are fully licensed, insured, and background-checked for your safety and peace of mind.

Do you offer any warranties on AC repairs?

Yes, we stand behind our workmanship and the parts we use with a comprehensive warranty. Specific terms will be provided with your repair service.

What can I do to prevent future AC breakdowns?

Change your filters every 3 months, keep the outdoor unit clear of debris, and schedule regular professional tune-ups to catch minor issues before they become major failures.

Can a dirty air filter really break my AC?

Yes. A clogged filter restricts airflow, which puts extreme strain on the blower motor and can cause your evaporator coil to freeze, potentially leading to compressor failure.

Why is my AC making a loud vibrating noise?

Loud vibrations can be caused by a failing fan motor, loose mounting bolts, or debris inside the outdoor unit. It's best to have it inspected before it causes further damage.

Is my thermostat the reason my AC won't turn on?

It's possible. A faulty thermostat, dead batteries, or bad wiring can prevent the signal from reaching your AC unit. We'll check the controls as part of our diagnostic process.
