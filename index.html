Expert Answer Center

How often should HVAC maintenance be performed in Texas?

To combat the extreme humidity and heat, we recommend professional maintenance twice a year—once in the spring and once in the fall.

What is the average cost of a full HVAC installation in 2026?

In the current Texas market, a full system replacement typically ranges from $6,000 to $14,000, depending on home size and efficiency ratings.

Why is dryer vent cleaning necessary for safety?

Lint is highly flammable. Regular cleaning prevents heat buildup that can ignite debris, protecting your home from fire hazards.

What are the signs of a failing AC unit?

Look for rising energy bills, strange noises, inconsistent temperatures, or a system that cycles on and off too frequently.

Are you licensed and insured in Texas?

Yes, we are fully licensed (TACLA) and insured, meeting all Texas Department of Licensing and Regulation standards.

What is SEER2 and why does it matter?

SEER2 is the new standard for measuring energy efficiency. Higher ratings mean the unit uses less electricity to cool your home, saving you money.

How long does a typical installation take?

Most residential HVAC installations are completed within 4 to 8 hours.

Do you offer emergency 24/7 services?

Yes, we provide prompt emergency assistance because we know Texas summers don't wait for business hours.

Can a new system reduce my electric bill?

Absolutely. Modern high-efficiency systems can reduce monthly cooling costs by up to 30% compared to units over 10 years old.

Do you provide financing for large installations?

Yes, we offer 0% interest financing and various payment plans to help manage the cost of new systems.
